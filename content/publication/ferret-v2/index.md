abstract: 'While Ferret seamlessly integrates regional understanding into the LLM to facilitate its referring and grounding capability, it poses certain limitations: constrained by the pre-trained fixed visual encoder and the097 limited image resolution, which negatively impacts its ability to process and interpret intricate visual details. In this work, we present Ferret-v2, a significant upgrade to Ferret, with three key designs. (1) Any resolution grounding and referring: Ferret-v2 can handle higher image resolution flexibly. (2) Multi-granularity visual encoding. (3) A three-stage training paradigm.'
